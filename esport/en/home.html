About us

PZR Esport is a team formed from a friendly group and community that came together many years ago, and since 2023, it has been competing in various virtual leagues. Its members have long proven their worth in the team, having achieved great results individually even before its establishment.

Our goal is, of course, to achieve victory and recognition in both domestic and international leagues and competitions. We want to represent ourselves in as many games as possible and build a team that strives for victory and can showcase outstanding results. Beyond competitions, our aim is to support each other's development through collaborative efforts.

"

My name is Martin Mózes, and my passion for racing began in childhood with the 2005 Formula 1 game. In 2014, I successfully swapped my keyboard for a steering wheel, marking the first milestone in my career. I participated in my first simulator race in 2017, which I managed to win. From that point on, it became a hobby and blossomed into a love. Since then, I have taken part in hundreds of races, both individually and as a team, all of which have been successful. Today, it has evolved into a passion, and I cannot settle for anything less than the top of the podium.

"
